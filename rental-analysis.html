Barcelona Rental Market Analysis

Goal: Understand rental value per district, supply hotspots, and price gaps between 1- and 2-bed flats.

Step 1: Data Collection

Collected rental data from Idealista for furnished flats under â‚¬2,000/month.

Step 2: Cleaning (SQL)


SELECT district,
       price,
       size_m2,
       price / size_m2 AS price_per_m2
FROM rentals
WHERE price < 2000;

Step 3: EDA (Python)


import pandas as pd
df = pd.read_csv('rentals.csv')
df.groupby('district')['price_per_m2'].mean().sort_values()
